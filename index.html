COVID-19 Impact on Travelers, Employee and Business
Python Pandas PySpark Matplotlib Seaborn

This project analyzes the impact of the COVID-19 pandemic on workplace mobility, stay-at-home requirements, and economic decline across different regions using PySpark and data visualization tools. Multiple datasets—including COVID-19 case counts, workplace visitor trends, stay-at-home mandates, and economic indicators—are ingested, cleaned, and merged using Spark SQL. The merged data is then converted to Pandas for in-depth analysis and visualization with Seaborn and Matplotlib. The project provides insights into how government policies and the pandemic affected workplace attendance and economic growth, presenting trends and distributions through time series plots and histograms.
